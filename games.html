About My Games

It is my belief that games are the ultimate form of art and storytelling, the bridge between the arts and technology. Programming, sound, visuals, story, and gameplay all come together to create a uniquely immersive experience. It's just one reason why I enjoy designing (and playing) games so much!

The majority of my games are currently built using Scratch, but I am in the process of porting them to Javascript and Python. I am also dabbling with the Unity game engine.

My games can be found at the following sites:

  • Scratch
  • Github
  • Gamejolt (WIP)
  • Newgrounds (WIP)
  • itch.io (WIP)
